# Daily Plans — Days 1–10 (Foundation + Verb Intro)
Go at your own pace. A "day" = one content unit. Each day: grammar first, then vocab, then kanji.

---

## DAY 1 — は + です (the skeleton)
**Grammar:** G01 です/だ, G02 は (topic marker)
**Vocab:** Part 1, words 001–020 (people, identity nouns)
**Kanji:** 一 二 三 四 五 (numbers 1–5)
**Produce:** Write 5 sentences: 「私は___です」(I am a ___). Then 5 「これは___です」.
**Anki:** Add all. Reviews start tomorrow.
**Immersion:** 15 min Japanese content, no pressure.

## DAY 2 — か + も + の (questions, also, possessive)
**Grammar:** G03 か, G04 も, G05 の
**Vocab:** Part 1, 021–040 (family, people)
**Kanji:** 六 七 八 九 十 (numbers 6–10)
**Produce:** Ask 5 questions 「___ですか」. Answer each. Use の in 3: 「私の___」.
**Anki:** New + Day 1 reviews.
**Immersion:** Read 3 signs/labels. Note any は or の.

## DAY 3 — SOV + を (word order, objects)
**Grammar:** G06 SOV word order, G07 を (object)
**Vocab:** Part 1, 041–060 (food, things you act on)
**Kanji:** 百 千 万 (big numbers) + 円 (yen) + 年 (year) [wait — see note]
> Kanji for Day 3: 百 千 万 円 — 4 kanji.
**Produce:** Write 8 sentences 「___を___ます」(verb in plain ます for now: 食べます/飲みます/見ます/読みます/買います).
**Anki:** New + Days 1–2 reviews.

## DAY 4 — が + は vs が (subject marking)
**Grammar:** G08 が, G09 は vs が
**Vocab:** Part 1, 061–080 (nature, existence subjects)
**Kanji:** 年 月 日 時 (time/calendar)
**Produce:** 5 sentences with が: 「___が好きです」「___があります」「誰が___」.
**Anki:** New + Days 1–3 reviews.
**Checkpoint:** Write 一〜十 from memory. If shaky, drill tonight.

## DAY 5 — に / で / へ (the location particles)
**Grammar:** G10 に, G11 で, G12 へ
**Vocab:** Part 1, 081–105 (places & transport)
**Kanji:** 分 半 週 曜 (time units)
**Produce:** 6 sentences. 2 with に (existence/time), 2 with で (action location), 2 with へ (direction).
**Anki:** New + Days 1–4 reviews.
**Immersion:** Walk somewhere. Read station/shop signs. Spot に and で.

## DAY 6 — と / や / ね / よ (connecting & nuance)
**Grammar:** G13 と, G14 や, G15 ね/よ
**Vocab:** Part 1, 106–125 (companions, objects, clothing)
**Kanji:** 午 間 大 小 (noon, interval, big, small)
**Produce:** 3 と-sentences (with someone / A and B), 2 や-sentences, 3 ending in ね or よ.
**Anki:** New + Days 1–5 reviews.

### ★ CHECKPOINT 1 (after Day 6)
- Build 「XはYです」 and 「XがYをVします」 freely?
- All 8 core particles (は が を に で へ の も) used right?
- ~25 kanji, ~125 vocab learned.
- If solid → continue. If shaky on particles → redo Days 4–6 before moving on.

---

## DAY 7 — verb groups + ます (the engine starts)
**Grammar:** G16 verb groups, G17 ます form
**Vocab:** Part 1, 126–140 (core verbs)
**Kanji:** 中 上 下 左 (position)
**Produce:** Take 10 verbs. Identify each group (1/2/3). Conjugate all 10 to ます form.
**Anki:** New + reviews.
**Note:** This is the most important week. Verbs unlock everything.

## DAY 8 — ません + dictionary form
**Grammar:** G18 ません/ませんでした, G19 dictionary form
**Vocab:** Part 1, 141–155 (more verbs)
**Kanji:** 右 前 後 外 (position/direction)
**Produce:** Conjugate 8 verbs into all four: ます/ません/ました/ませんでした.
**Anki:** New + reviews.

## DAY 9 — ない + た form (casual present neg & past)
**Grammar:** G20 ない form, G21 た form
**Vocab:** Part 1, 156–170 (daily verbs)
**Kanji:** 山 川 田 木 (nature)
**Produce:** Conjugate 8 verbs into ない and た. Watch う-verbs: 買う→買わない.
**Anki:** New + reviews.

## DAY 10 — て form + てください ★
**Grammar:** G22 て form, G23 てください
**Vocab:** Part 1, 171–185 (request-friendly verbs)
**Kanji:** 林 森 火 水 (nature)
**Produce:** Convert 12 verbs to て form. Then make 6 requests 「___てください」.
**Anki:** New + reviews.
**This is make-or-break.** The て form rules are mechanical — drill until automatic. Every G1 ending (く→いて, む→んで, etc.) must be reflexive. 行く→行って is the one exception; memorize it separately.

> Also study across days 1–10: adverbs (186–205), numbers/counters (206–247), question words/demonstratives (248–270) from Part 1. Sprinkle ~5/day into Anki.
